A fork of "https://github.com/drago-oo/lucid-theme"/ the excellent better lyrics theme, Lucid by drago-oo. 

This fork combines the beauty of the lucid theme with the Expressive typography of Google Sans Flex.


**RECOMMENDED:** It is highly recommended to enable `SHOW ON BROWSE PAGES` in the Better Lyrics Shaders setting for the best experience.

## Compatibility

- Works with Better Lyrics v2.0.5.6+
